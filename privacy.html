Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TurboWit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our applications and services, including CacheRecord and RetroSpecAna.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data (GPS)

CacheRecord uses your device's GPS to record driving routes. Location data is processed on your device and, when you choose to save a log, stored in our database associated with your account. We do not collect location data in the background without your explicit action to start recording.

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as app version, device type, and error logs to improve our services. This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.

Microphone / Audio

RetroSpecAna accesses your device's microphone solely to perform real-time audio spectrum analysis. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate our applications
  • To save and sync your drive logs across devices (when signed in)
  • To authenticate your account and maintain security
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send you important service-related notifications (if applicable)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service hosted in the Tokyo region. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

Guest mode data (when you use CacheRecord without signing in) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Vercel — Web hosting (vercel.com)
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — Map tiles and rendering (openstreetmap.org)
  • Mapbox — Map imagery for sharing cards (mapbox.com)
  • Google AdSense / AdMob — Advertising (google.com/adsense)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. Google's advertising services may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our web applications use localStorage to store your preferences (such as language settings) and session data locally on your device. We may also use cookies for authentication purposes. These are essential for the app to function and cannot be disabled.

6. Data Retention

Your account and associated data are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at hello@turbowit.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (drive logs can be exported as GPX files)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
